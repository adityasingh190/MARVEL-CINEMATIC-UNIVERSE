MAIN CHARACTERS


TONY STARK
Having created a wondrous suit of armor to keep himself alive,
Tony has revised it dozens of times,
each version with increased capabilities,
faster operation, reduced energy usage, and adaptability to the
greatest threats the universe has to offer.

STEVE ROGERS
Captain America is the alter ego of Steve Rogers,
a frail young artist enhanced to the peak of human perfection
by an experimental "super-soldier serum"
after joining the military to aid the United States
government's efforts in World War II.
